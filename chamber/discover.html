Discover Tokyo-3

It has been too many days since your last visit.

Welcome to Tokyo-3, where giant mechas roam the streets and apocalyptic events are a regular occurrence. Come witness the destruction and chaos, and experience the thrill of running for your life. Our city is the perfect vacation spot for adrenaline junkies and disaster enthusiasts. Don't forget to bring your survival gear! Book your trip now and enjoy the irony of a vacation destination that may just end up being your final resting place.

Despite the constant danger, Tokyo-3 is a vibrant and bustling city, filled with towering skyscrapers, neon lights, and crowded streets. The city has a distinctive, futuristic aesthetic, with sleek and angular architecture that gives it a unique look and feel. One of the most notable features of Tokyo-3 is the intricate network of underground tunnels and chambers that serve as the headquarters for NERV, the secretive organization tasked with defending the city against the Angels. These facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art technology and are manned by highly skilled pilots who operate giant, bio-mechanical weapons known as Evangelions.
