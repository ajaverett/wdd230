Design

I will be using a Skip Shade Gradient color palette that reflects the vibrant and dynamic world of Neon Genesis Evangelion. Our main color is a dark purple (#3F1043) which represents the dark energy and technology of the anime. This color will be used as the main background color for our website and as the primary color for our logo and buttons.

In addition to the neon blue, we have also chosen a dark grey (#333333) as our secondary color. This color will be used for the text and headings on our website to create a contrast and make the text easy to read.

To complement our color schema, we have also chosen a specific typography that reflects the futuristic and high-tech world of Neon Genesis Evangelion. Our main font choice is "Times New Roman MT Std", which is a modern and clean font that is perfect for creating a futuristic look and feel. This font will be used for all text and headings on our website.
